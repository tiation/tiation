How Prevention Works

Simple steps that Australian communities are using to prevent tragedies

1

Map Your Community

Identify neighbors who care about prevention and understand your community's unique strengths and challenges.

2

Listen & Learn

Host coffee sessions to understand what safety concerns keep your neighbors up at night and what solutions they'd support.

3

Plan Together

Design your first prevention activity - whether it's CPR training, men's connection groups, or resource sharing.

4

Take Action

Host your first event combining skill-building with community connection. Think BBQ with CPR training.

5

Grow & Share

Build on your success, train more neighbors, and inspire other communities to start their own prevention programs.

From Grief to Design

Start Good Now is part of the grief-to-design movement - transforming personal tragedy into systematic prevention. Instead of just responding to crises, we design communities that prevent them.

Prevention Over Response

Building community capacity to prevent problems instead of just responding to tragedies after they happen.

Community-Led Solutions

Local residents are the experts on their own communities. We provide support, not top-down programs.

Evidence-Based Impact

Everything we do is measured and proven. Real communities achieving real results that can be replicated.
